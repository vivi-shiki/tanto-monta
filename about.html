Tanto Monta is a card-driven strategy game set in the Iberian Peninsula during the late 15th century. Four powers — France, Castille & Aragon (Spain), Portugal, and the Islamic Maghreb — compete for control of key regions through diplomacy, warfare, and royal marriages.

Designer: Carlos Diaz (based on the "Here I Stand" system by Ed Beach).
